our projects and services.

Academic and Review Sessions

We hold academic and review sessions to help students study before their exams. In the sessions, we tackle lessons covered and address questions students have about the topics.

CODEX
Calendar of Deadlines, and Events and Exams

The Calendar of Deadlines, Events, and Exams or CODEX is a web-app that is developed by the CS Representatives for the reference of the entire DCS community. A valuable asset to the average student, the CODEX is used by many to stay up to date on all department-related events.

Troubleshooter

Troubleshooter is the helpdesk of the CS Representatives. Computer Science students may direct all of their departmental concerns to the CS Representatives anonymously.

Import *

To address the growing demand for course-specific review material, the Computer Science Representatives are developing a web-app similar to other educational apps like Duolingo. Primarily, majors from the department will be included, but will branch out eventually to electives such as Physics and Math. A team of developers will ensure that this web-app will be open to use for all students of the department.

Encrypt Locker System

The Encrypt Locker System is a free locker usage system for DCS students. The lockers shall help students store their projects in the department for a limited time.

C++
Community, Connection and Collaboration

In order to bring the department community closer together, a series of events and activities called the C++ Initiatives have been designed to foster holistic development and provide students and faculty alike with a venue to relax and focus on non-academic activities.

DCS Servers

Formerly known as the Dedicated Computer Science Students, the DCS Servers is the official volunteer corps of the Department of Computer Science. They organize the events of the department and cater to the needs of the Computer Science students. They also serve as an intermediary between the Students and the department's faculty.
